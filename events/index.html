Memphis AI Meetups & Events

Free weekly in-person meetups for Memphis business owners who want practical AI wins — no hype, no pitch.

WeAddBots hosts free, weekly in-person AI meetups at rotating locations across the Memphis metro area. Each meetup brings together local business owners, operators, and curious professionals for practical conversations about AI — what's actually working, what's not, and how to get started without getting overwhelmed. There's no sales pitch, no upsell, and no PowerPoint presentations. Just real people sharing real experiences over coffee or lunch.

The meetups rotate between six Memphis-area locations so you'll always have one close to home or work. Whether you've already built your first automation or you're still wondering what AI can actually do for a business like yours, you'll find the conversation useful.

What to Expect

What Happens at a Memphis AI Meetup?

💬

Practical Conversations

No keynote speakers or sponsor pitches. Just a group of local business owners talking about AI in plain English — what tools are worth trying, what problems they've solved, and what's still hype. Bring your questions.

🌎

Rotating Locations

We meet at a different spot across the Memphis metro each week — Collierville, Germantown, East Memphis, Midtown, Downtown, and Olive Branch. There's always one close to you, and you'll discover some great local businesses along the way.

🤝

Zero Pressure

These are community events, not sales funnels. Come for one meetup or come every week. Ask beginner questions or share advanced workflows. Everyone's welcome and nobody's keeping score.

Who Should Come?

The meetups are designed for Memphis-area business owners and operators — the people who run restaurants, service companies, retail shops, professional firms, and small teams. You don't need a technical background. You don't need to know anything about AI yet.

If you've ever thought "AI could probably help with this, but I don't know where to start" — that's exactly who the meetup is for. Most people who attend are in that same boat. That's what makes the conversations so useful.

You're also welcome if you're already using AI and want to compare notes with other business owners who are figuring this out in real-time.
