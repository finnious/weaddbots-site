What to Expect

Each meetup runs about 30 minutes. We keep it casual — no rigid agenda, no keynote, no slides. The conversation flows naturally based on what the group wants to talk about.

Typical topics include: which AI tools are actually worth paying for, how to automate specific tasks in your business, where AI falls short (it's important to know), what other Memphis businesses are doing with AI right now, and how to get started if you've been putting it off.

What you won't find: sales pitches, sponsor presentations, vendor demos, or anyone trying to sell you software. If someone asks about WeAddBots services, Scott answers honestly and moves on. The meetup is about the community, not the business.

Who Should Come?

Anyone who runs or operates a business in the Memphis area and is curious about AI. That's it. You don't need technical skills, you don't need to be using AI already, and you definitely don't need to understand how large language models work.

Most attendees are restaurant owners, service company operators, professional firm partners, retail managers, and solo entrepreneurs. The mix is what makes it valuable — someone running a dental practice might solve the same scheduling problem as someone running a catering company, but neither would have found the solution alone.
