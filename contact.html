What's the Free AI Assessment?

Here's the thing — most business owners know AI could probably help, but they don't know where to start. The assessment is designed to answer that question.

You'll tell us about your daily operations — the repetitive tasks, the bottlenecks, the things that fall through cracks. We'll identify the highest-impact spots where simple automation could make an immediate difference. No jargon, no upsell. If AI isn't the right fit for what you need, we'll tell you that too.

Quick Answers

💰 Cost to get started?

The assessment and initial call are free. Paid work starts at $300/hr for discovery sessions, $2k+ for implementation projects. No retainers.

🎯 Who's this for?

Business owners drowning in repetitive tasks, missed follow-ups, slow responses, or manual chaos. If you're already generating consistent activity but feel like you're leaving time on the table, this is for you.

🌎 Where do you serve?

US-based businesses. We're based out of Memphis, TN, and our in-person meetups are Memphis-area, but the automation work itself can be done remotely.

🛠 Do I need to replace my tools?

No. We layer automations on top of what you're already using. Google Workspace, your CRM, your email — they all stay. We just make them work smarter.
