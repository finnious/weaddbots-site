Privacy Policy

How we handle your information — in plain English.

Effective date: January 29, 2026

WeAddBots.com is operated by Creating Value LLC ("we," "us," "our"). This privacy policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and how we protect it. We've tried to keep this as clear and jargon-free as possible.

What We Collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out a form on our website — like the contact form, AI assessment request, or event RSVP — you provide information such as your name, email address, phone number (if you choose to include it), business name, and any details you share in the message field.

We store this information in our customer relationship management system (GoHighLevel) so we can respond to your inquiry, follow up appropriately, and keep track of our conversations. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information collected automatically

We use Clicky (clicky.com) for website analytics. Clicky collects information like which pages you visit, how you found us, your general geographic area, device type, and browser type. This helps us understand how people use the site so we can make it better. Clicky does not use cookies for tracking by default, and we do not run any advertising or retargeting scripts on this site.

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard server log information (IP address, browser type, page requested) as part of their hosting and security services. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, deliver services you've requested, send event-related communications (if you RSVP'd), and improve our website and services. If you contact us, we may follow up by email. We don't add you to a marketing email list unless you specifically opt in.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Service Purpose Data Processed
GoHighLevel CRM & form submissions Name, email, phone, message
Clicky Website analytics Page views, referrers, device info
Cloudflare Hosting & security Server logs, IP address

Data Retention

We retain contact information in our CRM as long as it's relevant to our business relationship. If you'd like your information removed, email scott@weaddbots.com and we'll take care of it promptly.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing scott@weaddbots.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

This website and our services are intended for business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we'll update the effective date at the top. For major changes, we'll make a reasonable effort to notify affected individuals.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email scott@weaddbots.com or use the contact form.
