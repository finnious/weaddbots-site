About WeAddBots

Every business now has access to an unlimited team. The question is: what will you have them work on?

Scott Finney

Founder, WeAddBots

Here's what I keep thinking about: for the first time ever, the business you built is no longer limited by the resources you wished you had.

Think about that for a second. Every small business owner I've ever met has a version of the same story — "If I just had one more person to handle the follow-ups." "If I just had someone to keep the books straight while I focus on clients." "If I could afford a marketing team." The answer was always the same: you can't. Not yet. Not at your size.

AI changed that. Not in a hype-cycle, buzzword-bingo kind of way. In a real, practical, right-now kind of way. You now have access to an unlimited number of skilled team members ready to be given direction on how to help your business achieve its goals. They work 24 hours a day. They don't call in sick. They cost a fraction of what you'd pay a human — and for a lot of tasks, they're just as good or better.

I spent 10+ years as a software engineer, building systems and solving problems with code. A few years ago, AI tools crossed a threshold that made me stop everything else. Because I realized: this isn't just another tech trend. This is the great equalizer. The same capabilities that Fortune 500 companies spend millions on are now accessible to a service company in Collierville, a professional firm in East Memphis, a retail shop in Bartlett.

That's why I started WeAddBots. To help Memphis business owners take advantage of this moment — practically, without the hype, and without ripping out what's already working.

A mindset I'll leave you with: What would you do if you had an army of 10,000 of the smartest people you've ever known willing to work for you 24 hours a day — what would you have them work on?

That's the question. And it's a real one now.

Our Approach

What We Believe

These aren't marketing slogans — they're the principles that guide every engagement.

🎯

Progress Over Perfection

Don't wait for the perfect AI strategy. Start with one automation that removes one friction point. Learn from it. Build from there. The businesses winning with AI right now didn't start with a master plan — they started with a first step.

🤝

Humans in the Loop

AI is a tool, not a replacement. The best automations keep humans where they matter most — making decisions, building relationships, doing creative work — and let AI handle the repetitive stuff. More humans doing human work, not fewer humans doing more.

💬

Plain English, Always

If we can't explain what an automation does in a sentence a 10th grader would understand, we haven't thought about it hard enough. No jargon. No buzzwords. No "leveraging synergies to unlock paradigm shifts." Just clear answers to real questions.

💪

Your Systems, Enhanced

We don't sell software. We don't lock you into platforms. We build automations on top of the tools you already use and trust. If we disappear tomorrow, your automations keep running. That's how it should be.

Quick Context: How We Actually Work

Every engagement starts the same way: a conversation. We learn about your business, your tools, your team, and — most importantly — the specific friction points that drive you crazy every day. No 40-page discovery documents. No death-by-PowerPoint.

From there, we map the simplest automation that solves the biggest problem first. We build it, test it, walk you through it, and make sure you understand exactly how it works. Then we ask: what's the next friction point?

That's it. No retainers unless results warrant it. No ongoing fees for the sake of ongoing fees. Just practical wins that compound over time.
