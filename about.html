About WeAddBots

AI is a big deal.

Not because it's trendy—because this is the first tool we've ever had that adapts to the user, instead of the user having to adapt to the tool.

WeAddBots exists to help Memphis-area business owners remove daily operational friction by designing and implementing simple, AI-powered automations—without ripping out existing systems.

We work with established small to mid-sized businesses already generating consistent activity but drowning in repetitive tasks, missed follow-ups, slow responses, and manual chaos.

Leaders who recognize AI could help but feel overwhelmed or unsure how to start—that's who we're here for.

Our Approach

We layer smart, lightweight automations on top of what's already working. We deliver practical "first wins" in days or weeks—not months-long transformations.

Local. Grounded. Proven—not hype.

A mindset to consider:

What would you do if you had an army of 10,000 of the smartest people you've ever known willing to work for you 24 hours a day—what would you have them work on?
