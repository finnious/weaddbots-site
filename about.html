About Us

More humans, not fewer.

We believe AI should free you up to do more of the work that matters—not replace the human touch that makes your business special.

Here's the thing about AI...

It's a big deal. Not because it's trendy—because this is the first tool we've ever had that adapts to the user, instead of the user having to adapt to the tool.

But here's what I keep seeing: business owners who know AI could help, but feel overwhelmed by where to start. They're drowning in options, jargon, and promises that sound too good to be true.

That's why I started WeAddBots. To cut through the noise and deliver something practical: automations that actually work, layered on top of systems you're already using, delivering wins in days—not months.

No rip-and-replace. No hype. Just progress.

What we believe

Progress over perfection

Local & grounded
