AI Automation Basics

Understanding AI Automation

AI automation uses artificial intelligence tools to handle repetitive business tasks automatically — things like responding to leads, routing inquiries, processing data, connecting tools, and generating reports. For small businesses, this typically means layering lightweight automations on top of existing tools like Google Workspace, CRMs, and accounting software. No custom software development or IT department required.

It usually starts with one specific friction point — like responding to leads faster, eliminating a repetitive data entry task, or connecting two tools that don't talk to each other. We use platforms like Zapier, GoHighLevel, and Google Workspace to build lightweight automations that run in the background. No custom software development, no IT department required. Most businesses see their first automation working within a week or two.
