How It Works

From Setup to Results in One Day

Rebecca isn't a six-month implementation project. It's a 30-minute setup and she's live.

1

30-Minute Setup Call

We configure Rebecca's parameters together — what information to gather from callers, what questions to ask, how to qualify leads, and where to route different types of calls. You tell us what matters; she learns it immediately.

2

Rebecca Starts Answering

Rebecca handles every inbound call: gathers caller information, asks your qualifying questions, filters out spam, and routes real opportunities to you or your team. Qualified leads get flagged instantly. Spam gets blocked silently.

3

Weekly Reports & Real-Time Alerts

Every week you get an email summary: who called, what they wanted, which leads are qualified, and what patterns Rebecca is seeing. Hot leads trigger real-time notifications so you can act fast. The monthly dashboard shows the full picture.

4

Monthly Check-In (15 min)

A quick monthly call to review performance, adjust Rebecca's criteria if needed, and make sure everything is dialed in. That's it — the total time commitment from you is about 25 minutes per month after setup.
